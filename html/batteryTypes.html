Battery Types

Every room in the home contains batteries. They are utilized in a variety of electrical and electronic products, including toys, doorbells, remote controls, mobile phones, and alarm clocks.

The majority of batteries are disposed of in landfills after being placed in trash bins. Batteries of all kinds, including lead, cadmium, zinc, lithium, and even mercury, may contain hazardous substances.

Batteries may leak these chemicals into the earth as they start to decay away in landfills, which can contaminate the soil and water.

Humans and the environment may be affected when chemicals pollute water and land creatures.

Recycling

Is a fantastic method to contribute to environmental protection. Each battery disposed of in a recycling bin will be disassembled, and the components will be repurposed to create new products.
